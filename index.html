Who Am I?

Media Technology and Sound Engineer

My name is Nino Prekratić and I am an engineer working with various media technologies. I have a big enthusiasm for technology in general, always looking for new tools and gadgets to blow my mind. I like to be surrounded by open and creative people with new ideas, who are always striving for self improvement.

I'm interested in interactive technologies incorporated in websites, applications, video games or art performances. I'm very excited about their potential and want to be a part of their evolution. Recording, editing, mixing and generating sound is my passion, and is something I feel very comfortable working with.

I am a big music fan with a broad teste, I love to play guitar, go for concerts or just listen to music on my own (preferably on good quality reproduction system ;) ). I will never say no to seeing a great movie in cinema or visting an interesting exsibition. I am very social, like to hang out and have a good time! Also, have I mentioned video games?! I love video games.

Skills

Main attributes

Interactive Technologies

Proficient in working with different interactive technology tools, such as Pure Data, Max 7, Processing 3 and p5.js. Good working knowledge of HTML/CSS/JavaScript, Bootstrap 4 and WordPress web technologies. Familiar with game development engines Unity 5 and Construct 2.

Sound Engineering

Comprehensive knowledge of recording, editing, mixing and mastering of sound. Well versed in sound synthesis and design. Familiar with speech synthesis, text to speech, dialog systems, speech and speaker recognition technologies. Worked with radio broadcasting and sound amplification systems.

Work Attitude

Hard working, organized and serious about his responsibilities. A team player who is always ready to go an extra mile. Always striving to improve and learn new things, in and outside of his area of expertise. Has service minded attitude and always eager to help. Adopts to new environments and challanges quickly.
